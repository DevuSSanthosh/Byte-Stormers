Foods to Eat During Your Period

During your menstrual cycle, your body needs extra care and nourishment. Here are some foods that can help alleviate symptoms and provide essential nutrients:

Dark Leafy Greens

Rich in iron, calcium, and vitamins A and C. These nutrients help replenish iron lost during menstruation and reduce inflammation.

Fatty Fish

High in omega-3 fatty acids, which can help reduce inflammation and alleviate menstrual pain.

Bananas

Rich in vitamin B6 and potassium, which can help reduce bloating and cramps.

Dark Chocolate

Contains magnesium and antioxidants that can help improve mood and reduce cramps.

Whole Grains

Provide complex carbohydrates for sustained energy and help regulate blood sugar levels.

Yogurt

Contains probiotics and calcium, which can help with digestive issues and reduce PMS symptoms.

Nuts and Seeds

Packed with healthy fats, protein, and minerals like magnesium and zinc, which can help with cramps and mood swings.

Berries

High in antioxidants and vitamin C, which can help boost your immune system and reduce inflammation.
